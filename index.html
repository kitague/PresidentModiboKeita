President Modibo Keita

First President of Mali

Background

Modibo Keïta was the first President of Mali and the Prime Minister of the Mali Federation. Born on June 04, 1915 Bamako, Mali. Married Mariam Traore. The Mali Federation was comprised of Senegal and the Sudanese Republic.The former Mali Federation was composed of the Republic of Senegal and the Soudanese Republic, both former colonies of France. On August 20, 1960, the Senegalese Assembly, after a series of political crises, passed a decree annulling its participation in the Mali Federation and denying any further competence over it by the Federation. The Government of Soudan immediately denied the lawfulness of this action, but the Government of Senegal claimed that its secession was legal. In late 1950's to early 1960's there have been a wave of Independence in West Africa. There was a Federation of Mali which comprised of Sudanese Republic and Senegal. Modibo Keita has been the first president of Federation of Mali with capital in Dakar, Senegal. The Federation of Mali was a did not last for long.

Quick Time line of President Modibo Keita life's

Modibo Keita was born in June, 4 1915 in Bamako Former French Sudan Capital
Modibo Keita his formal education at École normale supérieure William Ponty of Dakar capital of Senegal
In 1936 he was working as teacher in Bamako
In 1937 he was the coordinator of the art and theater group
In 1945 Modibo Keïta was a candidate for the Constituent Assembly of the French Fourth Republic, supported by GEC and the Sudanese Democratic Party
In 1945 Modibo Keita and Mamadou Konaté founded the Bloc soudanais, which developed into the Sudanese Union.
In 1946 he assumed the post of RDA Secretary-General in French Sudan, the African Democratic Rally (RDA) was created at a conference in Bamako
In 1948 he was elected councilor of French Sudan.
In 1956 Modi Keita was elected mayor of Bamako and became a member of the National Assembly of France
In 1960 July, 20 Modibo Keïta was elected constituent assembly president of the Mali Federation, which consisted of French Sudan.
In 1960 After the collapse of the federation, the US-RDA proclaimed the Soudanese Republic's complete independence as the Republic of Mali.
In 1961 President Modibo Keita paid a state visit to the United Kingdom, where Queen Elizabeth II invested him as an honorary Knight Grand.
In 1961 President Modibo Keita travelled to the United States of America in the company of Sukarno and met with President John F. Kennedy.
In 1964 He was relected as President of Mali.
In 1967 President Modibo Keita started the "revolution active" and suspended the constitution by creating the National Committee for the Defense of the Revolution (CNDR).
In 1968 President Modibo Keita was overthrew by General Moussa Traoré in a coup d'etat, and sent him to prison in the northern Malian town of Kidal.
